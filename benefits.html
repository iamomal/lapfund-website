The Benefits We Offer:

Retirement Benefits

A member is eligible for this benefit on attaining early retirement age of 50 years or the mandatory retirement age of 60 years. The benefits are payable either lump sum payment, annuity or income drawdown.

Emigration Benefits

A member is eligible to this benefit on leaving the country permanently without an intention of returning. The member is entitled to full payment of accumulated benefits.

Invalidity Benefits

A member is eligible for this benefit where he/she is certified to be permanently incapable of working because of physical/mental disability before attaining retirement age.

Survivor's Benefits

This benefit is payable to the nominated beneficiary(ies) upon death of member. The nominated beneficiary(ies) receive full payment of the members’ accumulated benefits.

Withdrawal Benefits

A member is eligible for this benefit on separation from the service of the employer through either resignation or dismissal before attaining early/mandatory retirement age. The member is entitled to 50% of his/her contribution and investment income accrued. The deferred benefits continue to earn investment income until the member attains early retirement age.

Pension Backed Mortgage

Use your savings to purchase a home

Members can use up to 60% of accumulated benefits to secure a mortgage(Purchase or improve a residential house).

Group Life Benefits

These are rider benefits that apply to members on permanent & pensionable terms and have been contributing to the Fund for at least one (1) year. They include:

  1. Funeral Expense: Equivalent to Kshs. 150,000 payable on death of a member.
  2. Death Benefit: Payable to beneficiary(ies) upon death of a member.
  3. Critical Illness: Payable to a member who suffers a critical ailment on first diagnosis.
  4. Permanent Total Disability: Payable to a member who suffers a disability as a result of an accident and has to separate from employment.
