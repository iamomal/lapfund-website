How do I join LAPFUND?

  • By filling an Admission form on our website – www.lapfund.or.ke
  • By Visiting our regional offices office.
  • Through enrollment by our representatives.

How do you tax the gratuity?

One of the benefits of Gratuity conversion is to enjoy the various tax relief.
The Gratuity will be taxed on a preferential rate for retirement benefits as opposed to the flat 30%.

Does the new NSSF ACT mean additional deductions from my payslip?

No. LAPFUND has contracted out of making NSSF Tier II contributions to NSSF. This means that the additional NSSF contributions will be part of the 12% you already contribute to LAPFUND.

Can I take an advance/loan on my contributions?

No. Under RBA regulations, the law does not allow your pension to be assigned to any liability.

If I get money from other sources, can I save it with LAPFUND and will it earn investment income?

YES. One is allowed and their savings will earn investment income.

How do I top up my contributions?

  • Check off (Payslip deduction)
  • Deposit money via MPESA (PAYBILL NUMBER 754488 and Account number: National ID number)
  • Standing order into our bank account (reference your name/National ID number)
  • Direct deposit into our bank accounts (reference your name/National ID number)

Will my voluntary contribution be subjected to double taxation?

No. There is no taxation on the principal contribution, but investment income shall be taxed.

How do I view my contributions/Statement online?

Via the LAPFUND website (members’ portal) or our USSD Code *632#.

Why is it important for the County Assembly Clerk or the County Secretary to clear MCA or Executive member during exit respectively (Voluntary)?

  • It is a requirement of LAPFUND procedure that a member who is attached to the employer be cleared/verified while accessing retirement benefits.
  • LAPFUND is an occupational scheme where members are attached to Employers.
  • An abundance of caution to ensure that the benefits are paid to the correct member.

Do you conduct member updates and education?

Yes. We provide regular updates though our Annual General Meetings(AGM). We also conduct customized sensitizations on personal financial plannings, preretirement, changes in pension industries among others.
