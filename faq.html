What is LAPFUND in full?

Local Authorities Provident Fund

How do you tax the gratuity?

One of the benefits of Gratuity conversion is to enjoy the various tax relief.
The Gratuity will be taxed on a preferential rate for retirement benefits as opposed to the flat 30%.

Why should the assembly remit the Gratuity to LAPFUND?

  • Members gain from the investment income over time.
  • Members will receive annual statements of accounts.
  • County assembly gains from LAPFUND's excellent track record in administration of retirement benefits, including prompt payment of benefits.
  • The responsibility of managing the county assembly gratuity fund will be passed to LAPFUND.
  • The County Assembly projects the image of a caring employer by ensuring that MCAs enjoy maximum benefits at the expiry of their term.

How does my remitted Gratuity earn investment income/return?

LAPFUND prudently invests in various asset classes including Government bonds and Treasury bills, Equities, Fixed Deposits, and Properties.

If I get money from other sources, can I save it with LAPFUND and will it earn investment income?

Yes, you are allowed.

  • Deposit money via MPESA PAYBILL NUMBER 754488 account number: National ID number.
  • Direct deposit into our bank accounts (reference your name/PF number).

Can I increase or decrease my voluntary contributions?

Yes. Members are advised/encouraged to periodically increase their voluntary contributions so as to maximize their savings.

Can the statutory deductions for the county government or assembly staff be adjusted from 12% to a rate that is higher or lower?

Yes. Based on an agreement (customized MOU) between the Sponsor and LAPFUND.

In case of death, resignation, or dismissal, what happens to my gratuity?

The nominated beneficiaries as per LAPFUND are paid equally or as per allocation done by the deceased.

  • Dismissal/degazettment - Member is paid.
  • Resignation - Member is paid.
  • Member/Beneficiary is mentally challenged/incapacitated - it is under the discretion of Trustees.

Why is it important for the County Assembly Clerk or the County Secretary to clear MCA or Executive member during exit respectively (Voluntary)?

  • It is a requirement of LAPFUND procedure that a member who is attached to the employer be cleared/verified while accessing retirement benefits.
  • LAPFUND is an occupational scheme where members are attached to Employers.
  • An abundance of caution to ensure that the benefits are paid to the correct member.

Will my Voluntary and Gratuity be subjected to double taxation?

No. There is no taxation on the principal, but investment income shall be taxed (Voluntary).
The Gratuity shall be taxed under the preferential graduated scale in a registered retirement benefits scheme.

Why is it important for the County Assembly Clerk or the County Secretary to clear MCA or Executive member during exit respectively (Gratuity)?

  • This is because the employer is remitting on behalf of the member. It is a direct benefit from the employer.
  • Abundance of Caution.

When is Gratuity Payable?

This is payable at the completion of term, resignation, dismissal, or death.
