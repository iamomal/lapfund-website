About Us

Who We Are

LAPFUND is a State Corporation established by an Act of Parliament Cap. 272, Laws of Kenya and a Defined Contribution Retirement Scheme registered and regulated by the Retirement Benefits Act of 1997 and Subsequent Regulations.

As a parastatal the Scheme is placed under the National Treasury, audited by the Office of the Auditor General and supervised by the Inspectorate of State Corporations.

Currently LAPFUND enjoys a membership of over 78,000 members.



Our Mandate

To provide savings and retirement benefits solutions to members of County Assemblies, County Executives, County Public Service, Members of staff of County Assemblies, Employees of water companies, Government agencies, Individuals and employees of private institutions.
