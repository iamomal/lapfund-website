Service Charter

LAPFUND has developed a comprehensive service charter that clearly outlines the range of services offered to its members in alignment with its mandate and the best practices within the retirement benefits industry. This charter serves as a guiding document to ensure transparency, accountability, and efficiency in service delivery.

By defining the scope of services available, the charter empowers you with the necessary information to understand the benefits and support you are entitled to as a member. It also establishes your rights and obligations, ensuring that you are well-informed about the processes and requirements involved when accessing LAPFUND services.

Additionally, the service charter sets clear expectations regarding service standards, turnaround times, and the level of professionalism you can anticipate from LAPFUND. Whether you are inquiring about contributions, withdrawals, pension benefits, or any other retirement-related services, the charter provides clarity on the procedures and expected timelines, fostering a more seamless and efficient experience.
